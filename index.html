The Firm

Security work, done with discipline

Precision Engagements is an independent cybersecurity consultancy serving federal agencies, government contractors, and private-sector organizations. We take on a limited number of engagements at a time and bring senior-level attention to every one — from the first scoping call to the final deliverable.

Clients come to us when the work matters, the details matter, and discretion matters. We plan thoroughly, report honestly, and finish what we start.

Capabilities

What we do

Security Assessments

Clear-eyed evaluation of your security posture: architecture reviews, control assessments, and gap analyses against recognized security standards. You get what is working, what is not, and what to fix first.

Incident Response & Digital Forensics

When something has gone wrong — or you suspect it has — we investigate: forensic collection and analysis, breach scoping, and clear reporting on what happened and what to do next.

Investigations & eDiscovery Support

Technical support for investigations and disputes: defensible data collection, analysis, and documentation that holds up under scrutiny.

Security Advisory & Program Delivery

Senior counsel for security decisions and steady hands for security initiatives. We help leadership plan the work, track it visibly, and drive it to completion.
